Wash and Fold

bubble_chart
If you are running short on time, or want to maintain further social distancing, drop off your laundry and we will weigh, sort, wash, dry, fold, and have it ready for pick up in 24 hours. Detergent, fabric softener, and dryer sheets are included. We wear masks and have a plexiglass shield for your protection. Our baskets and shelves are sterilized between customers. Drop off Sunday and pick up Monday, or drop off Wednesday and pick up Thursday.
