Safe and Convenient

wifi

All of our machines are viewable from the parking lot in front of the building. We have a large parking lot in the back of the building to accommodate trucks and RVs. We have free WiFi. The laundromat is at a convenient corner location. We are focused on keeping our prices low, while offering a safe and convenient experience.

COVID-19

As an essential business we have continued to offer our services to our customers during this pandemic. The laundromat is cleaned and sanitized daily. We ask our customers to please wear masks and to stay home if you are experiencing any symptoms of the virus or have been exposed.
